About · Who we are

We are Image Media

A team of professional sports photographers based in Europe, turning the energy of sport into images that last — frame by frame.

Our purpose

Turning fleeting moments into memories that inspire and endure

From marathons and trails to cycling races, triathlons and combat sports, we capture the spirit of every event. We know what athletes want — photos that show their effort, pride and achievement — and what organisers need: a reliable partner who adds real value. We work across Portugal, Spain, France, Italy, Ireland and the UK.

What we do

One studio, three jobs done well

Image Media is a sports-event media partner. Everything we do falls into three simple parts.

We cover the event

An on-site team shoots photo and short-form video, capturing as many participants as possible at their very best moments.

We publish the galleries

Photos go online the same day. Facial recognition lets every participant find themselves and buy their images in seconds.

We deliver value back

Organisers receive a ready-to-use content pack for marketing, sponsors and press — at no cost to their budget.

Our model

A partnership, not an invoice

We are funded by the participants who buy their photos — so coverage costs the organiser nothing, and can even earn them a commission. It is a model with three wins.

  • Marketing win — better content, more reach, a stronger brand.
  • Sponsor win — usable assets that sponsors actually repost.
  • Financial win — commission income, with no budget battle.
